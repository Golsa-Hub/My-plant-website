Plant pots are essential containers used to grow and support plants. They hold soil and
provide space for roots to grow properly. Pots are made from different materials such as clay,
plastic, ceramic, and metal. They come in various sizes and shapes to suit different types of plants.
Good plant pots help with drainage, plant health, and make indoor and outdoor spaces more beautiful.
