Soil and fertilizer play an important role in healthy plant growth. Soil provides a place for
roots to grow and holds water and air for the plant. Fertilizer adds essential nutrients that
soil may not have enough of. Together, they help plants grow stronger, greener, and healthier.
Using the right soil and proper fertilizer improves plant development and overall quality.
Good soil and fertilizer are key to successful gardening.
